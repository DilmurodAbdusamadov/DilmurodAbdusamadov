About Us

English Society is an enthusiastic community that aims to promote learning English. We welcome students from all classes with a love for excellence in the use of English. In the long term, we want to teach the maintenance, knowledge, development, and appreciation of the English language as used both in speech and in writing. We organize regular online and offline meetings, often with a guest speaker.
